## The Purpose of These Devices

Intermediary devices connect the individual [end devices](End%20Devices.md) to the network. They can connect multiple individual networks to form an [internetwork](Internetwork). These intermediary devices provide connectivity and ensure that data flows across the network.

Intermediary devices use the destination end device address, in conjunction with information about the network [interconnections](Network%20Media.md), to determine the path that messages should take through the network.

![[IntermediaryDevices.png]]

Intermediary network devices perform some or all of these functions:

- Repeat and retransmit communication signals
- Maintain information about what pathways exist through the network and internetwork
- Notify other devices of errors and communication failures
- Direct data along alternate pathways when there is a link failure
- Classify and direct messages according to priorities
- Permit or deny the flow of data, based on security settings

Not included in the intermediary devices is the **Hub** which basically serves the purpose of a multiport repeater only.
