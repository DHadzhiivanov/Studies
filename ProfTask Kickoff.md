# 2. Team Roles

## 2.1 **Architect** - David Hadzhiivanov

### 2.1.1 Task

Design the overall infrastructure, make technology choices, create network/system diagrams, ensure the design is consistent and scalable, and review technical work from other team members to make sure it aligns with the architecture.

### 2.1.2 How the role connects with others

- Works **closely with the Network Admin** - he designs it, the **Network Admin** builds it
- The **Security Officer** reviews the architecture for vulnerabilities - collaborate early
- The **Project Leader** will rely on his to estimate technical feasibility and timelines
- The **DevOps Engineer** implements what he specifies for deployment environments
- The **Scrum Master** will pull him into sprint planning to break his designs into tasks
- The **Privacy Officer** may flag design changes needed for compliance - they stay in sync
