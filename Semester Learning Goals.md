# Goal 1 - Strengthen core networking fundamentals through real case studies

## Target Outcome

Build a solid understanding of networking concepts by applying them to realistic scenarios. I should be able to explain how networks function, why specific design choices are made, and how different components interact.

### Actions

- Study core networking theory weekly.
- Apply concepts directly in case study scenarios.
- Practice subnetting, VLAN logic, and routing decisions.

### Success Criteria

- I can explain networking concepts without relying on notes.
- I make correct design decisions consistently.
- I understand the reasoning behind my configurations.

### Reflection (end of semester)

- What knowledge improved the most?
- Which topics still require work?
- How well did theory translate into practice?
