For all learning outcomes:

- That the learning outcomes relate to **infrastructure**. And by **infrastructure** we mean the set of ICT systems through which organizational processes are facilitated. This includes the traditional hardware infrastructure, but certainly also the software infrastructure as is common for organization similar in complexity to a SOHO. The end products are made according to standard methods.
- That you demonstrate competence in the learning outcomes in a **self-selected** format. By this is meant here that you can choose what evidence you provide, and choose what activities you perform to collect this evidence.
- That the learning outcomes are carried out in a **professional context**. These are the circumstances or situations, in which you find yourself working as a professional in the field of ICT Infrastructure.

## **Bachelor (Bsc.) & Associate Degree (AD)**

There are four learning outcomes related to knowledge and skills and two learning outcomes related to personal development. The following tables present the learning outcomes with a brief explanation.
